The Brief Overview behind the idea

Now, every world (Planet or Moon) that you can land on, will have a "Mission Hub" scene. These scenes are of that world in orbit, with a space station in the foreground. So if your last mission was on The Moon, then you would be orbiting the moon with a lunar space station, while you navigate the UI to pick a new mission. These backgrounds are also sort of alive. So for example, when the player picks and launches a new mission to play, the camera will pan to one of the ships docked on the station, as it undocks and leaves for its destination.

Rough version of the Earth Mission Hub

Remember I am only experimenting with this system, and it's likely to change and evolve a lot in the future.
